What products do you offer?

We offer perfume, body mist, lipstick, liquid lip color, balm stick, concealer, eyeshadow palette, sun stick, serum, cream, patches, skincare routine sets and beauty gift sets.

What are your reference prices?

Current website reference prices are perfume US$15 / pc, skincare US$1.50 / pc or pack, and lipstick / eyeshadow US$8 / pc or palette. Final quotation depends on quantity, packaging, formula and customization details.

Can I use my own logo?

Yes. We support private label logo customization on bottles, tubes, labels, cartons, gift boxes and sample kits. You can send your logo or reference image through WhatsApp.

Can you customize perfume fragrance?

Yes. We can discuss fragrance direction such as floral, fruity, rose, vanilla, musk, amber, oud, fresh, sweet or unisex styles. Exact sample options should be confirmed by WhatsApp.

Can you customize makeup shades?

Yes. Lipstick, liquid lip color, balm stick and eyeshadow palettes can be discussed by color family, shade range, texture and packaging style.

Can you customize skincare formula?

Yes. Skincare customization can include texture, packaging, positioning and ingredient direction. Regulatory requirements vary by target market, so final claims and labels should be reviewed carefully.

Do you support small orders?

The website is designed for wholesale and private label inquiries. Small trial orders or sample kits can be discussed through WhatsApp. MOQ depends on product type and packaging choice.

How do I request samples?

Click any Request Sample or WhatsApp Quote button and send your product category, target quantity, country and packaging needs. We will guide you through sample options.

Can you make gift sets?

Yes. We can discuss perfume discovery sets, makeup starter sets, skincare routine sets, body care sets and private label sample kits.

How long does sampling take?

Sampling time depends on product type, logo process, packaging materials and formula complexity. We recommend confirming the specific product first on WhatsApp.

How long does bulk production take?

Bulk production time depends on quantity, packaging, product complexity and order schedule. We will confirm a practical timeline after product and packaging details are clear.

Can you help with packaging design direction?

Yes. We can work from your logo, color preference, target customer and reference images to suggest bottle, tube, box and gift set packaging direction.
