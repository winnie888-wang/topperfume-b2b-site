Warranty coverage

We provide quality support for confirmed manufacturing defects, wrong product styles caused by our production error, serious leakage caused by packaging defects, and quantity shortage confirmed by inspection records.

  • Perfume bottle, spray pump and cap functional issues caused by production defects
  • Skincare or makeup packaging defects affecting normal sale or use
  • Wrong logo, wrong carton or wrong shade confirmed against approved artwork/sample
  • Bulk order quality issues reported with clear photos, videos and carton labels

Not covered

Because most B2B goods are customized, some situations are not covered by standard warranty.

  • Customer-approved artwork, spelling, color or packaging choices
  • Damage caused by customer-side storage, customs inspection or local handling
  • Normal slight color difference between screen display, sample and bulk production
  • Issues caused by unauthorized resale, repacking or formula changes after delivery

How to report a quality issue

Please contact us within 7 days after receiving goods for visible damage or shortage, and within the agreed warranty period for production defects.

Required proofOrder number, product name, photos, videos, carton labels and quantity affected
Review timeUsually 2–5 working days after receiving complete evidence
Possible solutionReplacement, credit for next order, partial compensation or other agreed solution
For customized products, the approved sample and final artwork are the main quality reference.
