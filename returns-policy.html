Customized order returns

Private label perfume, skincare, makeup, cartons, labels, bottles, tubes and gift sets are usually customized. After artwork/sample approval and production start, customized orders cannot be returned or cancelled for non-quality reasons.

  • Logo, packaging, shade, fragrance or formula customized for your brand
  • Products manufactured after sample or artwork approval
  • Goods already shipped or accepted by buyer/forwarder

Return or replacement may be accepted when

  • Wrong product shipped due to our confirmed mistake
  • Wrong logo, wrong carton or wrong shade compared with approved production file
  • Serious production defect confirmed by photos, videos and inspection review
  • Quantity shortage confirmed by packing record and carton evidence

Possible solutions include replacement, remake, partial credit, discount on next order or other agreed solution.

How to request return support

TimeframeContact us within 7 days after receiving goods for visible damage, wrong goods or shortage
EvidenceOrder number, product name, issue description, photos, videos, carton label and affected quantity
ConditionGoods should be unused, unopened where possible, and kept in original packaging
Shipping costReturn/replacement shipping cost is confirmed after responsibility review
Please do not return goods without written confirmation from our sales team. Unauthorized returns may not be accepted.
